TimePlayed

The easy-to-use Discord bot
that measures your playtime

Hi there, I am TimePlayed

I am a bot that helps you as server owner to view what games are popular in your server, and to get users to be active in your server. I'm measuring your playtime using Discord presences, so you and your server members will have an overview about how long and which games they play.
It's quite useful, you should definitely give it a try!

Features

  • Compatible with any application

    I am compatible with any game/software you can think of. I simply work with Discord presences, so the only thing you'll have to do is add a game (tutorial here), and run your Discord while gaming. Then I'll log your playtime!

  • Free for everyone

    All my essential commands/features (except some special add-ons) are, and will always be, entirely free. I'm relying entirely on donations of users.

  • Privacy friendly

    Users will individually need to accept to my Terms of Service in order to log their playtime, I will not log anything without anyone's permission

  • User friendly

    I'm always improving the bot's user experience to keep it as simple and understandable as possible. I listen to your feedback and try to make the bot perfect.

  • Always online and available

    Except for some updates or unexpected issues, I'm always there for you. Reach out to me whenever you need me, 24/7 all days of the year!

  • Many useful commands!

    I have a ton of features, with a lot of commands. Check out the command page to get a list of all of my amazing commands!
